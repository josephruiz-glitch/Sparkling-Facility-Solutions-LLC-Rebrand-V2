Our Story

Built for the Commercial Market. Focused on Results.

Sparkling Facility Solutions was founded in the Florida Keys with a simple belief: commercial clients deserve a cleaning partner that operates like a business, not a side hustle. From day one, we built systems, trained crews, and pursued the credentials that commercial buyers require to trust a vendor with their facilities.

Over the years, we've expanded our footprint across Florida's major markets, developing expertise in post-construction cleaning, multi-site facility janitorial programs, and commercial turnover services. Our clients include general contractors, property management groups, corporate facility teams, and commercial developers from the Keys to Jacksonville.

Today, Sparkling Facility Solutions is repositioning as Florida's premier commercial cleaning and post-construction specialist — bringing futuristic tools, streamlined communication, and field-tested expertise to every project we take on.
