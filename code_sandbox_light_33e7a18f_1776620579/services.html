Post-Construction Cleaning

Florida's construction boom demands cleaning partners who understand jobsite protocols, punch-list standards, and occupancy timelines. We've handled post-construction cleans for developers, general contractors, and tenant improvement projects from Key West to Jacksonville — delivering inspection-ready spaces every time.

Our post-construction cleaning process is structured in phases that align with your project timeline. Whether you need a one-time final clean or a full three-phase program (rough, final, and touch-up), we coordinate directly with your project manager to work around active trades and hit occupancy deadlines.

Phase 1 — Rough Clean
  • Remove large construction debris
  • Sweep and vacuum all floors
  • Wipe down surfaces for trade access
  • Clear dumpsters and debris piles
  • Initial wall and ceiling surface wipe
Phase 2 — Final Clean
  • Full dust removal, HEPA vacuuming
  • Window & glass label/sticker removal
  • All surface detail cleaning
  • Restroom sanitization & polish
  • Floor clean & initial polish
Phase 3 — Touch-Up Clean
  • Pre-walkthrough inspection clean
  • Final glass and mirror polish
  • Spot cleaning & dust re-check
  • Fixture detail & polishing
  • Quality control sign-off sheet
Specialty Scope Items
  • HVAC grille & ductwork wipe-down
  • Protective film removal
  • Concrete dust & grout haze removal
  • Construction adhesive cleanup
  • Exterior entry & pressure washing
